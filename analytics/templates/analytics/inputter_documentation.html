Inputter Documentation

The inputter enters information observe on the field into the provided fields as part of the data collection gathering.

Procedure

  1. Login to the app
  2. Select a module from the list. This is what you would like to fill information on
  3. Fill in details of the form

  4. You can include a picture of any observation made
  5. Submit form when you have filled in all the details you need to.
